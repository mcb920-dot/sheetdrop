Features
Everything raw Excel
was never meant to be

SheetDrop turns cold, hard spreadsheet data into something you're actually proud to show your boss.

✨
Instant beautification
Drop your file and watch it transform in milliseconds. Clean typography, proper spacing, smart number formatting — all automatic.
🔍
Filter any column
Click the filter button under any column header to drill into exactly what you need. Stack multiple filters across columns simultaneously.
↕️
One-click sorting
Click any column header to sort ascending or descending. Numbers, text, and currency all sort intelligently.
💰
Smart number formatting
Currency columns are automatically detected and formatted with $ signs and commas. Numbers are right-aligned and color coded for instant readability.
🔎
Global search
Search across all columns at once with instant highlighting. Find any value in thousands of rows in milliseconds.
🔒
100% private
Your data never leaves your device. Everything runs locally in your browser — no uploads, no servers, no accounts required.
